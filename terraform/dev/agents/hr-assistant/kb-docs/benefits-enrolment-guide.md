# Benefits Enrolment Guide

**Policy Owner:** HR Department
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2025
**Review Date:** 31 December 2025
**Classification:** INTERNAL

---

## Overview

The company offers a comprehensive benefits package to all permanent employees. Benefits are administered through the company benefits portal (link on the intranet).

---

## Core Benefits (Automatic)

All permanent employees automatically receive:

- **Pension:** 5% employee contribution, 5% employer contribution (auto-enrolled on day 1). Employees may increase their contribution at any time.
- **Life Assurance:** 4x annual salary (death in service benefit)
- **Income Protection:** 75% of salary after 26 weeks of continuous sickness absence
- **Private Medical Insurance (PMI):** Individual cover from day 1. Family cover available as a flexible benefit.
- **25 days annual leave** (plus UK bank holidays) — see Annual Leave Policy

---

## Flexible Benefits (Opt-In)

During the annual benefits window (October–November each year), employees may choose from the following:

| Benefit | Details |
|---|---|
| Family PMI | Add partner and/or children to private medical cover |
| Dental insurance | Annual dental cover (two tiers available) |
| Critical illness cover | Lump sum payment on diagnosis of covered conditions |
| Cycle to Work | Save tax and NI on bikes and equipment up to £2,000 |
| Technology scheme | Spread salary sacrifice over 12 months for tech purchases |
| Additional pension | Increase pension contributions via salary sacrifice |
| Additional annual leave | Buy up to 5 extra days leave per year |

---

## New Starter Enrolment

New starters have **60 days** from their start date to make flexible benefit elections. After 60 days, changes can only be made during the annual benefits window or following a qualifying life event (marriage, divorce, birth of child, etc.).

---

## Annual Benefits Window

The benefits window opens in **October** each year for changes effective from 1 January. Reminders are sent via the HR portal and company email.
