# Remote Working Policy

**Policy Owner:** HR Department
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2025
**Review Date:** 31 December 2025
**Classification:** INTERNAL

---

## Hybrid Working Model

The company operates a **hybrid working model**. Employees are expected to work from the office at least **3 days per week**. The remaining days may be worked remotely, subject to role requirements and manager approval.

Some roles are designated fully office-based or fully remote — these are specified in the employee's contract.

---

## Eligibility

All permanent employees who have completed their probationary period (typically 3 months) are eligible for hybrid working arrangements. Employees during probation are expected to work fully in the office.

---

## Equipment and Expenses

The company provides:
- Laptop (standard specification or approved upgrade)
- Headset for video conferencing
- Access to required software and systems

Employees working from home are responsible for their own broadband connection. A **broadband allowance of £30 per month** is available upon request and approved by the line manager — claim via the expenses system.

A one-off home office equipment allowance of **£200** (chair, desk lamp, monitor stand, etc.) is available to employees with a formal hybrid working agreement. Receipts must be submitted within 60 days.

---

## Health and Safety

Employees must complete a remote working risk assessment before starting regular home working. The assessment is available on the HR portal. Employees are responsible for ensuring their home working environment is safe.

---

## Data Security

When working remotely, employees must:
- Use only company-approved devices and VPN
- Not work from public Wi-Fi without VPN
- Follow the Information Security Policy at all times
- Not access or process company data on personal devices

---

## Flexible Start and Finish Times

Core hours are **10:00–16:00** Monday to Friday. Outside core hours, employees may adjust their start and finish times by agreement with their manager.

---
