# Parental Leave Policy

**Policy Owner:** HR Department
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2025
**Review Date:** 31 December 2025
**Classification:** INTERNAL

---

## Maternity Leave

Eligible employees are entitled to up to **52 weeks of maternity leave** (26 weeks Ordinary Maternity Leave + 26 weeks Additional Maternity Leave).

**Company Enhanced Maternity Pay:**
- Weeks 1–16: full pay
- Weeks 17–26: half pay
- Weeks 27–39: Statutory Maternity Pay (SMP) at 90% of average weekly earnings for the first 6 weeks, then £184.03/week (2025 rate) or 90% of average weekly earnings (whichever is lower)
- Weeks 40–52: unpaid

Eligibility requires 26 weeks of continuous service by the qualifying week (15 weeks before the expected week of childbirth).

---

## Paternity Leave

Primary partners (not taking maternity leave) are entitled to:
- **2 weeks of paid paternity leave** at full pay, to be taken within 56 days of the birth or adoption placement.

---

## Shared Parental Leave (SPL)

Eligible parents can share up to **50 weeks of leave** and up to **37 weeks of Shared Parental Pay** (ShPP). The enhanced pay described for maternity leave applies to shared parental leave where the primary carer has transferred leave entitlement.

---

## Adoption Leave

Employees adopting a child are entitled to the same leave and pay as maternity leave (up to 52 weeks). The leave begins on the day of placement.

---

## Keeping in Touch (KIT) Days

Employees on maternity, adoption, or shared parental leave may work up to **10 Keeping in Touch days** without losing their entitlement to statutory or enhanced pay.

---

## Returning to Work

Employees returning within 26 weeks of maternity leave are entitled to return to the same job. Those returning after more than 26 weeks are entitled to the same or a suitable equivalent role.

Flexible working requests submitted on return from parental leave are given priority consideration.
