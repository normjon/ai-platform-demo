# Employee Assistance Programme (EAP)

**Policy Owner:** HR Department
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2025
**Review Date:** 31 December 2025
**Classification:** INTERNAL

---

## What is the EAP?

The Employee Assistance Programme (EAP) is a free, confidential support service available to all employees and their immediate family members (spouse/partner and dependants living in the same household).

The EAP is operated by an independent third-party provider and is completely confidential — no information about individual usage is shared with the company.

---

## How to Access

**Phone:** **1800-EAP-HELP** (free, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year)

**Online portal:** Available via the HR self-service portal under "Wellbeing & Support"

**App:** Download the EAP app (details on the HR portal) for on-demand access to resources.

---

## What Support Is Available?

The EAP provides access to:

- **Short-term counselling** — up to 8 sessions per issue per year with a qualified counsellor (face-to-face, telephone, or video)
- **24/7 telephone counselling** — immediate emotional support at any time
- **Mental health support** — stress, anxiety, depression, bereavement, trauma
- **Financial guidance** — debt management, budgeting, benefits advice
- **Legal advice** — employment, family, consumer, property law (not a substitute for formal legal representation)
- **Childcare and eldercare** — information and referrals
- **Nutritional and fitness advice**

---

## Confidentiality

All calls and sessions are completely confidential. The EAP provider will not disclose any information to the company without explicit written consent from the employee, except where there is a serious risk of harm to the employee or others.

---

## If You Are in Crisis

If you or a colleague is in immediate danger, call **999**.

For urgent mental health support outside of a life-threatening emergency, call the EAP on **1800-EAP-HELP** or the Samaritans on **116 123** (free, 24/7).

---

## Manager Support Line

Managers have access to a dedicated manager support line through the EAP for guidance on handling sensitive employee situations, managing team wellbeing, and making referrals.
