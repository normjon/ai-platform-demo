# Expenses Policy

**Policy Owner:** Finance Department / HR Department
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2025
**Review Date:** 31 December 2025
**Classification:** INTERNAL

---

## Overview

This policy covers the reimbursement of reasonable out-of-pocket expenses incurred by employees in the course of their work.

---

## Approval and Submission

All expenses must be approved by the employee's line manager before submission. Expenses must be submitted within **60 days** of the expense being incurred via the expenses management system (link on the company intranet).

Receipts are required for all items over £10.

---

## Travel

**Mileage (own vehicle):** Reimbursed at **45p per mile** for the first 10,000 miles per tax year, then 25p per mile, in line with HMRC advisory rates.

**Rail travel:** Standard class. First class is permitted for journeys over 3 hours with prior manager approval.

**Air travel:** Economy class for flights under 5 hours. Business class permitted for flights over 5 hours with director approval.

**Taxis and car hire:** Reimbursed where public transport is impractical or unavailable.

**Parking:** Reimbursed with receipt.

---

## Accommodation

Hotel accommodation is reimbursed up to:
- **£150 per night in London**
- **£120 per night elsewhere in the UK**
- **£200 per night internationally**

Breakfast included in the hotel rate is covered. Separate breakfast claims are not permitted if breakfast is available at the hotel.

---

## Subsistence

| Meal | Allowance |
|---|---|
| Breakfast (if required to leave before 7:00 am) | £10 |
| Lunch (if away from base for more than 5 hours) | £15 |
| Dinner (if required to stay overnight or return after 8:00 pm) | £30 |
| 24-hour subsistence maximum | £55 |

---

## Entertainment

Client entertainment requires prior manager approval and must be submitted with a business justification. The maximum is **£75 per head** without director approval. Alcohol is included in the per-head rate but excessive spend will not be reimbursed.

Staff entertainment (team lunches, etc.) requires manager approval up to £25 per head.

---

## Non-Reimbursable Items

The following are not reimbursed: personal items, fines, minibar costs, in-room movies, gym fees, personal travel, commuting costs.
